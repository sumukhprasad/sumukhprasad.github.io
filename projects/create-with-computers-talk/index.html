Create with Computers

"Create with Computers" was a talk given by Mr. Prasanth Nori (prasanthnori.com) of the Scratch team at DPS Bangalore South.

Mr. Nori walked the students through a problem solving session on Scratch and taught them how to approach problems with the right mindset, by using the example of making a sprite walk from left to right. He also introduced them to Scratch Labs, where students can use experimental Scratch features such as video sprites and face recognition.
